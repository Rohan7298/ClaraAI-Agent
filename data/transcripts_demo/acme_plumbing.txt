Demo call with Acme Plumbing

Caller: Hi, this is John from Acme Plumbing. We're a plumbing company serving residential customers.

Agent: Great, what are your business hours?

Agent: How do you handle emergencies?

Caller: For water leaks or burst pipes, callers should be transferred immediately to our on-call plumber. If the first person doesn't answer within 30 seconds, try the backup number, then take a message.

Agent: What's your office address?

Caller: 123 Main Street, Springfield, IL 62701.

Agent: Any special rules for non-emergency calls?

Caller: During business hours, route to our dispatcher who schedules appointments. After hours, take a message and they'll get a call back next business day.
